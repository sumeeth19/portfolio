1. Business Problem

Business stakeholders lacked a unified analytics solution to track revenue performance, customer behavior, and product trends over time. Reporting was manual, inconsistent, and time-consuming.

2. Project Objective

  • Create a clean, analytics-ready dataset
  • Define standardized business KPIs
  • Enable MoM and YoY trend analysis
  • Deliver executive-ready dashboards

3. Architecture & Data Flow

  • Raw sales data ingested into Google Cloud Storage
  • Data cleaned and transformed using BigQuery SQL
  • Curated analytics views created for reporting
  • Power BI connected to BigQuery views

5. Data Transformations

  • Filtered invalid quantity and price values
  • Converted order_date to DATE format
  • Derived Year, Month, and Year-Month fields
  • Calculated revenue and discount metrics
  • Applied customer and product segmentation

6. Sample SQL Logic

SELECT
  EXTRACT(YEAR FROM order_date) AS order_year,
  EXTRACT(MONTH FROM order_date) AS order_month,
  SUM(total_amount) AS total_revenue
FROM sales_clean
GROUP BY 1,2;

7.Key Metrics & Measures

Key metrics were derived from cleaned BigQuery tables and implemented as Power BI measures to support business performance analysis.

Metric Source Purpose
Total Revenue SUM(final_amount) Overall sales performance tracking
Total Orders COUNT(order_id) Volume analysis
Average Order Value (AOV) Revenue / Orders Customer spending behavior
MoM Growth % DAX Time Intelligence Month-over-month trend analysis
YoY Growth % DAX Time Intelligence Year-over-year comparison
